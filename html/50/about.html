Perched on the mountainside, where the air is crisp and the view stretches endlessly,
Mnt Olympus is more than just a Greek restaurant—it’s an experience that transports you to the heart of Greece.
Inspired by the rich culinary traditions and legendary hospitality of the Mediterranean, we offer an authentic taste of Greece with a backdrop that’s nothing short of divine.

From our fresh, locally sourced ingredients to our time-honored recipes passed down through generations, every dish at Mnt Olympus is crafted with passion.
Whether you're savoring our perfectly grilled souvlaki, indulging in layers of flaky baklava, or toasting with a glass of ouzo, each bite is a celebration of Greek heritage.

At Mnt Olympus, we believe that food is best enjoyed in good company.
Whether you're sharing stories over a family-style feast or enjoying a romantic dinner under the stars, our warm and welcoming atmosphere makes every meal unforgettable.
So, ascend to new culinary heights and join us for an experience where tradition meets breathtaking scenery.
